About Satbhav

Satbhav is the technical association for students in the Department of Computer Science and Applications, Amritapuri Campus. The main motive of the association is to help students improve and nurture their ideas and also to provide them a platform for learning about the latest technologies by conducting hands-on workshops, technical events and talks by our own alumini
